Why Allelio?

700M+ DNA Tests Sold

Millions of people have taken consumer DNA tests to explore their ancestry and health insights.

All Require Data Upload

Every mainstream service requires uploading your raw genomic data to their servers, raising privacy concerns.

No Open Alternative

Until now, no privacy-preserving, open-source tool existed for analyzing your own genomic data locally.

Features

100% Local Processing

Your genome data never leaves your computer. Complete privacy and control.

AI-Powered Insights

Plain-English explanations of genetic findings via local LLM integration.

Multiple Formats

Support for 23andMe, AncestryDNA, VCF, and other common genomic formats.

Research-Backed

Powered by ClinVar and GWAS Catalog databases for accurate variant annotation.

Beautiful Reports

Interactive web UI with exportable HTML reports for easy sharing with healthcare providers.

Open Source

MIT licensed and community-driven. Transparent, auditable, and extensible.

How It Works

1

Install

Get Allelio running with pip in seconds.

pip install allelio
2

Setup

Download research databases locally (one-time).

allelio setup
3

Launch

Start the web interface on your machine.

allelio serve
4

Analyze

Upload your DNA file and explore your insights.

localhost:8080

Install Allelio and start analyzing your genome in minutes.
